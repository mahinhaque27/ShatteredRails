HELP PAGE FOR SHATTERED RAILS!

Shattered Rails 1:

In Shattered Rails 1, you will be prompted to click buttons whenever a prompt arrives.

There'll be a text prompt that looks like this:

"You are in a strange space and you see two directions around you, do you want to go left, or right?"

-----------------------------------

Options: z = Go left / x = Go right

-----------------------------------


Some times you will be prompted to engage in battle. There are two actions for these: Attack and stay still.


In the beginning of the game you are prompted for difficulty of the game, easy and hard choices are easy and hard respectively, making your health lower and the enemy's health higher the higher difficulty you go, whilst medium is a good middle of the line option. When you go down certain paths and make certain decisions, you can unlock special/good/bad endings, and you are encouraged to explore all the paths available to you.


You can use the customization options above and below the terminal to enhance your experience. These options include changing font size/color/family, or zooming into the terminal/zooming out. You can change the color of the terminal/background if needed as well. Text to speech and speed of text are also options that can be changed to suit your needs.

Shattered Rails 2:

In shattered Rails 2, you will be prompted to click buttons whenever a prompt arrives.

There'll be a text prompt that looks like this:

"Enemy attacks you!"

-------------------------------------------------------------

Options: z = Attack / x = Defend / c = Stay Still / v = Spell / N = Run/ P = Persuade

-------------------------------------------------------------


In this game there are no path options, only battle options.


Your goal is beat the enemy, get their health to 0.


In the beginning you will be prompted 2 things, for the game's difficulty and the user type. Game's difficulty option is the same as Shattered Rails 1. User Type is something that's new. The user can choose between 4 character types that have different attributes: Knight is the default balance, Thief has less health but can evade enemy attacks easier, Berserker is the highest damage and health, but cannot evade as easily as the other types, Magician's spell is much stronger than any other type's.


There are 6 different actions you can take in this game. Attack/Defend/Stay Still/Spell/Run/Persuade. Attack lets you attack the enemy and do damage to them. Defend gives the user to either defend complete damage or a fraction of the enemy's attack based on a percentage. Stay still regains 5 MP to the user every time it used, but leaves the user vulnerable for a turn. Spell gives the user the opportunity to cast a spell, which is 2 times as strong as a regular attack, but consumes MP(magic points), which is max 10 points for everyone except the Magician who has 20 MP as a max. Run lets you run away from the battle. Persuade lets you talk to the enemy and leads to a chance of averting a battle.


Your goal is beat the enemy, get their health to 0. You will fight the soldier of whichever minion you chose, before fighting said minion.


You can use the customization options in the settings button to enhance your experience. These options include changing font size/color/family, or zooming into the terminal/zooming out. You can change the color of the terminal/background if needed as well. Text to speech and speed of text are also options that can be changed to suit your needs.
